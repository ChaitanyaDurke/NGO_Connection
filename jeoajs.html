About NGO Volunteer Connect

At NGO Volunteer Connect, we are dedicated to bridging the gap between volunteers and NGOs to bring about meaningful change. Our mission is to create a thriving network of passionate individuals and organizations working towards a better world.

Our Journey

Founded in 2019

We began with a vision to create a platform where passionate individuals could easily connect with NGOs, driving impactful social changes.

Expanded in 2021

We expanded our reach to include more NGOs and volunteers, ensuring we cover a diverse range of causes and needs.

Now Serving Worldwide

Today, we connect thousands of volunteers and NGOs across the globe, fostering collaboration for social good.
